A four-app suite under one roof, built for Christian families who want their kids to enjoy media without the algorithmic minefield. **SafeTube** — a curated, ad-free YouTube replacement with parent-set channel allowlists. **SafeTunes** — kid-safe streaming music that fixes the parental-controls gap Apple Music never closed; clean lyrics, vetted artists, family-paced playlists. **SafeReads** — a kid-safe ebook reader with classic and contemporary books vetted for content. **SafeStudy** — homework help that won't drift into anything inappropriate. Single-source JWT auth across all four apps, one family code, one subscription. Stack: Next.js (marketing + SafeTube + SafeStudy), React + Vite (SafeTunes + SafeReads), Convex across five deployments, Stripe billing, custom content-vetting pipeline. Live, paying customers, founded and operated solo.
