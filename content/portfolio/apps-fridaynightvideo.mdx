A nostalgia game that captures the magic of walking into Blockbuster on a Friday night in the late '80s and '90s — the carpet, the wall of VHS tapes, the snack aisle, the slow scan of new releases. Browse, rent, and recreate the ritual. A love letter to the era that shaped how a generation watched movies. Stack: Next.js, Vercel.
