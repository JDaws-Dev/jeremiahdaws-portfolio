End-to-end video production and social-media marketing for a private performing-arts school in metro Atlanta. Twenty-two theatrical productions I produced, directed, or edited — full musicals (Big Fish, Beauty and the Beast, Little Women, Jane Eyre), short films, behind-the-scenes documentaries, dance and choir recitals — plus the school's Instagram content arm: high-view-count theater BTS, opening-night reels, recital sneak peeks, fundraiser pieces, and the year-end recap the school explicitly credited me for. I also built and run the school's film program, teaching the students who help produce this content.
