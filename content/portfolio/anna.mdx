A household assistant for my wife Michelle, modeled on Anna Bates from *Downton Abbey* — warm, deferential, attentive, never performative. ~240 tools across email triage with learned senders, calendar, a Convex-backed budget, homeschool, church, family logistics, hosting prep, and proactive memory using topic extraction and FTS recall. Mood-aware engagement: daily message budgets, do-not-disturb windows, conversational permission instead of approval-button cards. Shares a household SQLite store with JARVIS so they answer with one voice on the kids, the calendar, and the family rhythms — and stays out of Jeremiah-private context. Forked alongside JARVIS from a shared base; differentiated by `SOUL.md` + `PROFILE.md` persona files that hot-reload per prompt.
