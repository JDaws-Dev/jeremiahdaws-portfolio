3D-scanned, modeled, printed, painted, and wired the candle hands and head for Lumière in a high-school production of “Beauty and the Beast”. The kind of stage-reliability tuning that only matters when a kid is on stage and the candles need to actually light. Storytelling, fabrication, and teaching all in one prop.
