How it works

Three steps. That's the whole flow.

No course to enroll in. No video to scrub through. Pick it up for five minutes a day, or an hour at a time — whatever works.

1
Pick a name
A first name and a password. That's your account. Type the same name on any other device and your progress follows you.
2
Tune and tap
Open the mic-based tuner, get your strings in tune, then tap your first chord — Em. Two fingers, two seconds, you're playing.
3
Master, then play
Mark chords as you learn them. Songs unlock as soon as you know the chords they need. By chord four, you can play "Ho Hey."
What's inside

A whole guitar lesson in a single page.

Everything a beginner needs — and not much more.

Mic-based tuner
Pluck a string and the needle tells you sharp or flat. Auto-detects the closest string. No clip-on needed.
Seven open chords
Em, Am, D, G, C, E, A — every one with a fingering diagram, the notes labeled, and tap-anywhere audio.
Fifteen lessons
From "meet your guitar" all the way to chord families and capo theory. Tap, hear, and explore — not walls of text.
Practice tools
Metronome with tap-tempo, five strum patterns synced to BPM, a chord-switcher drill that times you, and an ear-training game.
Real songs & riffs
28 chord charts (Ho Hey, Wagon Wheel, Riptide…) and six iconic riffs in TAB — from Mary Had a Little Lamb to Iron Man.
Synced progress
Your chords, streak, achievements, and session plan save automatically and follow you to any device — phone, tablet, laptop.
