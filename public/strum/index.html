What's inside

A whole guitar lesson in a single page.

Everything a beginner needs — and not much more. No videos to wade through, no ads, no subscription.

Mic-based tuner
Pluck a string, the needle tells you sharp or flat. Auto-detects the closest string. No clip-ons needed.
Seven open chords
Em, Am, D, G, C, E, A — every one with a fingering diagram, the notes labeled, and pluck-anywhere audio.
Fifteen lessons
From "meet your guitar" to chord families and capo theory. Tap, hear, and explore — no reading walls of text.
Practice tools
Metronome with tap-tempo, five strum patterns synced to BPM, and a chord-switching drill that times you.
Real songs & riffs
28 chord charts (Ho Hey, Wagon Wheel, Riptide…) and six iconic riffs in TAB — from Mary Had a Little Lamb to Iron Man.
Progress that syncs
Pick a name and password. Your chords, streak, and badges follow you to any device — phone, tablet, laptop.
